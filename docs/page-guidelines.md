## **Purpose**

**This document explains how secondary pages should be designed and structured in the Okelcor website.**

**The screenshots provided are taken from the current Okelcor website, but the goal is not to replicate them exactly.**

**Instead, they provide content structure and functional reference, while the visual design should follow Tesla-inspired UI principles defined in `design-system.md`.**

**The pages covered here are:**

* **Shop**  
* **Product Detail**  
* **Checkout**  
* **About**  
* **Contact**  
* **News**

---

# **Global Page Design Rules**

**All pages must follow the Tesla-inspired layout system.**

**This means:**

**• Strong typography**  
**• Large whitespace**  
**• Clean minimal sections**  
**• Clear hierarchy**  
**• Bold hero sections**  
**• Large imagery where possible**  
**• Smooth scrolling layout**

**Avoid:**

**• crowded layouts**  
**• dense grids**  
**• small cramped typography**

**Each page should feel premium, modern, and spacious.**

---

## **Purpose**

# **Design Reminder**

**These pages should follow Tesla-inspired layout principles, not traditional ecommerce UI.**

**Priorities:**

* **minimalism**  
* **strong imagery**  
* **large typography**  
* **generous spacing**  
* **premium feel**

**Okelcor Orange should be used primarily for:**

* **primary buttons**  
* **CTA elements**  
* **highlights**
